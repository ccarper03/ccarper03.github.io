    Casino Bonus Round

    This mini game is the bonus round you get to play inbetween casino slot games.

    Made this in four days on my own. I learned how to use the DOTween plugin where you can animate coins dynamically. Created an algorithm to lose and win a percentage of the time. Modified distortion script to get the effect I wanted with the water. Made particle effects for the bubbles. Utilized asset packs for the artwork. Created most of the sound effects.
